Dakonte Product Group (DPG) is a leading manufacturer and seller of sewn and heat seal vinyl products for customers in the US and Canada. With over 35 years of experience, DPG offers a wide range of products under three brands: TIE Officemates™, Great Menu Covers™, and EASY Partitions™. DPG serves various industries, such as manufacturing, sales, supply chain management, restaurant and hospitality, and minority and woman-owned business. DPG is committed to providing high-quality products and customer service, as well as complying with all the applicable laws, regulations, standards, and ethical practices.
